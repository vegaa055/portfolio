About Me

I am a developer who is optimistic about the possibilities of breakthroughs possible through innovation and creativity in the fields of software and game development. At an early age, I was fascinated by science, technology, and video games.

I enjoy the challenge involved in trying to solve a particular problem using logic and code. And lots of caffeine! There is something so rewarding in that moment you get something to work properly.

I started college a little later in life. For so long, I felt as if that were something that was beyond me – that I wasn’t good enough. However, I eventually reached a point to where I told myself I was going to give it my all and do everything I can to become successful at something. Maybe I hadn’t been good enough; but if I work that much harder, become the best version of myself I can be, then how could they deny me. My plan was to become so good at what I do that I couldn’t be denied.

I began to set small goals that built up bigger goals. As I started to get good grades something happened, it motivated me even more. Soon I was making the Dean’s list, President’s list and winning scholarships. The best part for me, however, was everything that I was learning. Being able to create something so complex from nothing but code and logic is extremely rewarding.
