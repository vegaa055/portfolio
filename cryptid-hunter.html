Game Overview

Cryptid Hunter: Rogue is a turn-based RPG where players explore procedurally-generated dungeons, battling legendary monsters and collecting loot. The game features a dynamic combat system that allows players to strategize and adapt to different enemy types.

Players take on the role of a cryptid hunter, venturing into dark forests and ancient ruins to uncover the secrets of legendary creatures. With each dungeon run, players face new challenges and discover unique loot that enhances their abilities.

The game is designed to be replayable, with each dungeon offering a unique layout and set of enemies. Players can customize their characters with various skills and equipment, allowing for diverse playstyles.

Game Story

In a world where legendary creatures roam the shadows, players become cryptid hunters, tasked with uncovering the mysteries of these elusive beings. As they delve into dark forests and ancient ruins, they encounter legendary monsters that test their skills and courage.

The game unfolds through a series of procedurally-generated dungeons, each filled with unique challenges and treasures. Players must strategize their approach to combat, using a dynamic system that rewards tactical thinking and adaptability.
